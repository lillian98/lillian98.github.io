How to request for a Return & Refund

JD Collection Products

  • 1Email to JD

    Email postsales@JD.com to send a Return &

    Refund Request.

  • 2Write a Brief Reason

    In the email, please provide clear pictures of the item, the shipping label, and write a brief reason for returning the item.

  • 3Await Response

    After you have done this, await further instructions from our customer service team. They will respond to you within 2 business days

Third-Party Seller Products

  • 1Contact the Seller

    If your order hasn't arrived or isn’t as described you can contact the seller. To do this, go to “My Account” and select “My Order”. Click “contact us” next to the item you want to return to resolve this directly with the seller.

  • 2Create a Refund Request

    If you were unable to resolve the problem with the seller, you can submit a refund request to JD’s Customer Service Team by clicking the “Refund” button next to the item you want to return in the “My Order” page.

  • 3Open Dispute

    If your Refund Request was denied, you can open a dispute by selecting “Dispute” in the “My Order” page.
